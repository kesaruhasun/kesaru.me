About Me

I’m Kesaru Hasun, an IT Support Executive at Register.lk based in Meegoda, Sri Lanka. I completed my primary education at Army School Panagoda and secondary education at MRC Homagama, graduating in the Mathematics stream. Currently, I’m pursuing my passion for Artificial Intelligence at SLIIT. I have a strong interest in Networking, Server Management, Automation, and Machine Learning. My fascination extends to Physics, Astrophysics, Science, Mathematics, and Data Science. Problem-solving and resilience are my strong suits, and I love exploring new ideas and technologies.

I also take pride in my soft skills: I was the Quiz Master of my school’s quiz club and have experience comparing (emceeing) school events. I enjoy writing blog posts for Register.lk to share insights on tech and other exciting topics.
